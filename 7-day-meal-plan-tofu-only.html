7‑Day High‑Protein Meal Plan (Tofu‑Only)

2 meals (lunch + dinner) and 2 snacks per day. Targets: ≤ 1,100 kcal and ≥ 120 g protein per day (estimates). Meals are designed for < 15 minutes with repeatable prep. No tempeh used.

Nutrition figures are estimates based on typical values and the labels visible in your photos. If your tofu brand differs materially, treat calories/protein as directional and adjust portions. Egg is not used as a main ingredient (and not used in these meals).
